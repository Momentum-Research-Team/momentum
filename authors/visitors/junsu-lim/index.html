Junsu Lim

Visiting Member

MOMENTUM Lab, Seoul National University

Junsu Lim is a Visiting Member at MOMENTUM Lab, Seoul National University, majoring in Big Data Convergence at Sangmyung University. He is a key researcher within the SUDAL Medical Imaging Team. His research interests focus on medical imaging, generative AI, diffusion models (including LLDM), vision-language models (VLM), and transformer-based architectures for advanced medical diagnostics.

He actively collaborates with Samsung Medical Center radiologists and NVIDIA researchers to develop clinically robust AI systems for automated healthcare diagnostics and medical image computing, with a strong focus on high-fidelity MRI and anatomical reasoning.
