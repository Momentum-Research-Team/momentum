Nam-Joon Kim

Principal Investigator

MOMENTUM Lab, Seoul National University

Nam-Joon Kim is the Principal Investigator of MOMENTUM Lab at Seoul National University. His research focuses on intelligent multimedia signal processing, artificial intelligence, computer vision, and advanced machine learning for real-world multimedia applications.

Before founding and leading MOMENTUM Lab, he built extensive experience across academia and industry, including research positions at LG Electronics, Samsung Advanced Institute of Technology, and Samsung Electronics. His work spans deep learning, pattern and visual recognition, multimedia systems, and AI hardware.
