MOMENTUM, Sangmyung University

Professor Aeri Lee is the Faculty Advisor for the MOMENTUM Research Team at Sangmyung University. She guides the student research groups in machine learning, business analytics, and data mining, promoting excellence in both academic and industry-level data science projects.
